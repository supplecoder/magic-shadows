Nobody knows why she has more magic than any regular elf, and honestly? She's not complaining. At thirteen, a half-elf betrayed the kingdom and it fell to Moon to stop them. She did. Now at sixteen she's still figuring out the full scope of what she can do. As a full elf she can shift into any animal form, but her favourite is always the fox 🦊 — the form even half-elves can take, and the one that feels most like her.
